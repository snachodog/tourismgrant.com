Major Project

Montana Dinosaur Center Gallery Expansion

In Progress Choteau Area Community Tourism Grant

Financial Summary

Budget & Spending

The Montana Dinosaur Center is expanding its gallery to display a seismosaurus model and other specimens currently held in storage. The success of our public-facing digs has made this expansion both necessary and exciting — we need new gallery space to grow our collections and our reach. The new facility will provide a unique venue for guest speakers, youth and adult educational events, and traveling exhibits, attracting a greater variety of visitors and increasing time spent at one of the region's premier destinations.
