Major Project

Old Trail Museum – Rocky Mountain Front Interpretive Center

In Progress Choteau Area Community Tourism Grant

Financial Summary

Budget & Spending

The Old Trail Museum is constructing a new multi-purpose building to house the Rocky Mountain Front Interpretive Center — a multidisciplinary interpretive space developed in collaboration with Montana Fish, Wildlife & Parks, scientists, experts, and outdoor recreationalists. The center will exhibit the unique natural history, ecology, and diverse wildlife of the Rocky Mountain Front, and will serve as a visitor center hub, event center, and community meeting space for educational programming and lecture series.
